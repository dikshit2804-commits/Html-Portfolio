The best movies according to Dikshit Joshi

My top 3 movies of all time are as follows:

Interstellar

Directed by Christopher Nolan, truly a masterpiece that explores space and time.

Inception

Another Christopher Nolan classic that bends the mind with its concept of dreams within dreams.

The Dark Knight

The epitome of superhero movies, with an unforgettable performance by Heath Ledger as the Joker.
